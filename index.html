"We strive to make customers happy by making fresh, delicious protein smoothies."

Chlomoney's Protein Spot was founded on May 2nd, 2019 and it's only location is in Atlanta. Chloe Scheffler grew up in a healthy lifestyle and discovered that she loved making smoothies. She spent her highschool and some college career working at Smoothie King and Tropical Smoothie. She also loved serving within the community and helping others. This didn't stop her from owning her own business. After 2 years in college, she decided to open her own protein business based in Atlanta. Ever since then, her business has been growing and growing. There's nothing that feels good more than making people feel better and happier than before.
